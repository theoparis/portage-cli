# Stage 3e — post-solve within-run BDEPEND trim (2026-06-16)

## Change

After `install_order`, `bdepend_trim::trim_within_run_bdepend` walks the plan
with the same BROOT growth model as `preflight`, dropping entries only needed
for `BDEPEND` already satisfied on host/prefix VDB or earlier merges.
No-op when `--with-bdeps` is off.

## Cross parity (riscv64-unknown-linux-gnu, sys-devel/gcc)

| Mode | em | emerge |
|------|-----|--------|
| default | 18 | 18 |
| --with-bdeps | 18 | 18 |

Trim does not change cross gcc closure (already correct after 3d).

## Wall time (hyperfine, 3 runs)

| Case | mean ± σ |
|------|----------|
| default `-p` | 634.8 ms ± 79.8 ms |
| `--with-bdeps` | 636.5 ms ± 31.5 ms |

Overhead vs 3d (~614 ms): within noise. Trim metadata walk is cheap relative
to PubGrub solve; primary win is plan-size parity on long `--with-bdeps`
prefix chains, not cross timing.
